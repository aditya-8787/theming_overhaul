How It Works

Three-Layer Architecture

Mapped from reading controllers.py directly. Each layer is independent — changing your Bootswatch skin doesn't reset your chapter colours.

01
ESP Theme
Controls page layout — sidebar, navbar placement, jumbotron structure. Preserved exactly as-is. No new themes needed.
droplets bigpicture circles floaty fruitsalad barebones
02
Bootswatch Skin NEW
Colour and typography preset injected into the LESS pipeline before chapter overrides. 26 skins available. Selectable from the theme editor.
Flatly Lux Minty Cyborg Sketchy +21 more
03
Chapter Overrides
Individual chapter branding saved as Tag key-value pairs. Applied last — wins over everything. Colours, fonts, spacing all customisable.
@accent1 @headingColor @footerBackground @h1FontSize @jumbotronPaddingV
